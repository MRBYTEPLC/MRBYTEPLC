### 🎯 About Me

Building **open-source solutions for Smart Manufacturing** — PLC programming, Industrial IoT, SCADA, and Python-powered MES tools that bring Industry 4.0 within reach of small manufacturers and startups.

- 🏭 Focused on **Industrial Automation**, **OEE Tracking**, and **PLC ↔ Python integration**
- 🛠️ Building affordable, **open-source MES tools** for small manufacturers
- 📺 Teaching automation and Python on **[YouTube — @MrBytePLC](https://www.youtube.com/@MrBytePLC)**
- 🌐 Portfolio: **[mrbyteplc.github.io](https://mrbyteplc.github.io/)**
- 🤝 Open to collaborating with people who share the same passion for innovation
